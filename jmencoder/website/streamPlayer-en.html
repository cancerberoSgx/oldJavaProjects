This is a radio and tv stream player. This program includes

  • A catalog with more than 10000 radios of all over the world broadcasting over the internet.
  • tv channel playlists from all over the world
  • Easy to edit, create, save, load channel categorized playlists
  • Support for complex streaming playlist formats like asx, pls, m4u, ram, etc

Usage

There are available several playlist, for example: radios from europe (all countries), radios from europe categorized, radios from north america, south america and others.
